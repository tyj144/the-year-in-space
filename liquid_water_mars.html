The moons of Mars, Phobos and Deimos, are named after the two horses which pulled the chariot of the Roman god of war Mars.

On September 28th, 2015, NASA announced one of the most incredible discoveries of the year: liquid water on the planet Mars.

Found by NASA's Mars Reconnaissance Orbiter, the liquid water on Mars flows intermittently, usually when temperatures are above -10 degrees Fahrenheit. The water remains a liquid at this temperature due to the hydrated salts that dramatically lowers its freezing point.

Many scientists hope that this will lead to the possibility of life being found on Mars.
